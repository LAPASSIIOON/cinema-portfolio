Increase Salon Bookings with a Seamless Online Reservation System زد حجوزات صالونك بنظام حجز إلكتروني سلس

Problem المشكلة
Customers searched for "barber near me" and found nothing. Walk-ins dropped because there was no way to browse services or book ahead. The owner watched competitors take appointments from people who would have chosen his salon — if they had found it online. Low online trust meant lost revenue every single week.
Solution الحل
A premium bilingual landing page with WhatsApp booking, Google Maps sync, service gallery, and mobile-first design — delivered in 4 days. One site that works like a 24/7 sales person.
Result النتيجة
Bookings now come in directly through the website and WhatsApp — 24 hours a day, even when the salon is closed. New customers trust the brand instantly because it looks professional online. More appointments booked. Less missed revenue. The salon finally looks as premium online as it feels inside.

✦ Retail MVP

Cut Order Errors and Speed Up Restaurant Operations with a Smart Dashboard قلّل أخطاء الطلبات وسرّع عمليات مطعمك بلوحة تحكم ذكية

Problem المشكلة
Orders were handwritten and easily lost. Staff spent hours counting inventory by hand. Profit calculations took forever and were often wrong. Mistakes meant refunds and unhappy customers — wrong orders, stock running out unexpectedly, long wait times. The owner had no real-time view of daily sales or stock. Running the business was guesswork.
Solution الحل
A complete management dashboard with smart inventory tracking, instant profit analytics, automated order processing, and a unified backend system — all in a custom MVP built from the ground up.
Result النتيجة
Order processing sped up significantly. Profit and stock data is now available instantly — no more manual counting or late-night spreadsheet sessions. Errors dropped, customers are served faster, and the owner finally has full control. The business runs smoothly and is ready to scale without adding chaos.
